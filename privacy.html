Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data on our educational platform

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At EduEarn India, we take your privacy seriously. As an educational platform, we collect minimal data necessary to provide and improve our free educational content.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Contact Form Data: When you contact us through our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content to respond to your educational inquiries.
  • Newsletter Subscription: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address to send educational updates.

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with our educational content, including pages visited, time spent, and referring websites.
  • Technical Data: We collect browser type, device type, IP address (anonymized), and operating system for analytics purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our educational content
  • Google AdSense to display relevant, non-intrusive advertisements
  • Remembering your preferences (like dark mode if implemented)

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Responding to your educational inquiries
  • Improving the quality of our educational content
  • Sending educational newsletters (only if subscribed)
  • Analyzing website performance and user engagement
  • Displaying relevant advertisements to support free education

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our website (like hosting providers, analytics services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Third-Party Services

Our educational platform uses these third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding how visitors use our content
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements to support free education
  • Font Awesome: For icons used throughout the website
  • Google Fonts: For typography on our website

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

As a user of our educational platform, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of newsletter communications
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings

Children's Privacy

Our educational content is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@eduearnindia.com
  • Use our contact form

Important Note

This privacy policy applies only to our educational website. When you click on external links (tools, resources, affiliate links), you will be subject to the privacy policies of those third-party websites.
